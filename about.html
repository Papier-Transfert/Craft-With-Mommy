About

The crafts are simple.
The memories are forever.

Hi! I'm Chloé, the mom behind Craft with Mommy.

I became a mom and suddenly I understood what everyone meant when they said "they grow up so fast."

One afternoon, I looked up from my phone and realized my kids were on theirs too. We were in the same room and completely disconnected.

That was my wake-up call.

I wanted us to DO something together. Something hands-on. Something we'd actually remember. But every craft idea I found online was either too complicated, too messy, or way too stressful for a regular Tuesday afternoon.

I'm not an artist. I'm not a Pinterest-perfect mom. I'm just a mom who wanted to look at my kids and say "let's make something together."

So I started experimenting. Simple projects. Easy materials. No special skills required.

And something magic happened: my kids lit up. We laughed. We got a little glitter on the table (okay, a lot). And at the end, they held up their creations with the biggest smiles and said "Look what WE made, Mommy!"

That moment? That's everything.

That's why I created Craft with Mommy.

Every craft on this site follows four simple rules.

🎨

No talent required

Every project is designed so you (and your kids!) can't mess it up.

⏱

Quick & easy cleanup

From setup to done in under an hour, without dreading the mess.

👶

Made for little hands

Crafts tested for ages 2–8, with or without fine motor skills.

💛

Screens off, smiles on

The real goal? A moment of joy, laughter, and connection.

This is for you if…

  • You want to make memories with your kids, not just keep them busy
  • You've tried complicated crafts and ended up stressed (same!)
  • You don't consider yourself "crafty" at all
  • You want something cute and easy that your kids will actually finish
  • You're ready to put the phone down for 30 minutes of real family time
"Crafts are not just a way to keep kids busy. They are a way to create real moments of connection. We're here to make those moments as simple, joyful, and memorable as possible."

Chloé, founder of Craft with Mommy
