Questions & Answers

Your questions, answered

Everything you need to know before you pick up the scissors and the glue stick.

Craft with Mommy is a blog dedicated to simple DIY projects you can do with your little ones. You'll find paper crafts, handprint crafts, paper plate crafts, and more, all with step-by-step instructions.

Every craft for kids is designed to bring joy, giggles, and a cute result you'll want to display at home. 😜

Not at all, and we truly mean it. Every craft is foolproof, with clear step-by-step instructions made for beginners.

You don't need any artistic talent, and neither do your kids. If you can use scissors and a glue stick, you're ready. The goal isn't perfection, it's the smiles along the way.

Most of our crafts are perfect for children ages 2 to 10.

Toddlers and preschoolers can enjoy simple projects side by side with Mom, turning craft time into a sweet moment of connection and shared memories. 💛 Feel free to adapt any craft to your child's level, that's part of the fun.

Nobody wants to spend more time cleaning than crafting. Most projects are low-mess and won't turn your kitchen table into a disaster zone.

Plus, every blog post includes a messiness rating, so you always know what to expect.

Most crafts take between 15 minutes and one hour. Perfect for a free afternoon, a rainy morning, or a quick after-school activity.

Each blog post shows the estimated time so you can choose the right project for your schedule. ✅

Most crafts use everyday supplies you probably already have: paper, glue, scissors, markers, and paint.

Each blog post includes a clear supply list, and if you're missing anything, we've added handy Amazon links so you can grab everything in a few clicks.
